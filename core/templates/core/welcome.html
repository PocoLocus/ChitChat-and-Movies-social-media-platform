Welcome to ChitChat

This is a social media platform. In the main ChitChat tab you can share your thoughts, comment on and like other's posts, explore user profiles, sort posts by tags and search by author or content. There is an activity feed section to keep you updated for the latest comments. In the top-right corner of the navigation bar you can visit your own profile and the settings tab, where you can update your username or your password. We have recently introduced the Movies tab, where users can search for films via the TMDB API, rate them and add them to the shared movies list. Each movie entry includes detailed information. Other users can view, rate and update their ratings for any added movie.
